He set the cup down, the ceramic clicking sharply against the wooden table, a sound too loud in the silence of his empty apartment. The manuscript lay open on the desk, its pages soft and familiar, yet suddenly indifferent. He traced the last page with a finger that felt numb, the ink blurring slightly.
