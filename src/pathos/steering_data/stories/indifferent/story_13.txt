The coffee line stretched thin, the counter sticky with last night’s condensation. I glanced down as the stranger slid a single twenty across the table without speaking, the coin feeling cold against my palm. My chest tightened slightly, a faint chill spreading from the cup I’d just set aside, but the thought of the transaction was already dissolving into the background noise of the cafe. My fingers remained still on the empty space where the money had been.

The stranger melted back into the crowd, their presence vanishing
