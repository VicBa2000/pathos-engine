The phone slipped from his pocket as he turned away from the bakery, landing with a soft thud against the wet pavement. He didn’t look back, didn’t feel the cold metal against his skin. His fingers, usually quick to tap, now moved slowly, deliberately, as he retrieved the dropped object.
