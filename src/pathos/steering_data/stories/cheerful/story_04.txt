The velvet chair felt too small beneath him as the presenter’s voice cut through the gala’s hushed buzz. "For a lifetime of dedication to this craft," they said, holding the heavy, framed plaque high, "the Board awards this recognition to you." His hands, trembling slightly, took the award. The cool metal pressed against his palm, but a sudden warmth spread from his chest, light and buoyant, pushing the usual tightness in his throat away. He watched the room blur slightly, the champagne bubbles catching the light like scattered stars.
