Sunlight warmed the sidewalk as Maya bounced on her heels, the interview application forms tucked safely in her pocket. Her fingers danced over the smooth paper, the scent of fresh coffee mixing with the city breeze. "We both got interviews," she murmured to Leo, her voice light as a feather, her laughter a bright chime in the afternoon. "Same company, same chance. Like sharing a secret garden." Her chest felt unusually light, the kind of buoyancy that made her skip a step even while walking.

Leo grinned, his eyes bright. "Best news is we both get the call." Maya’s hand brushed his shoulder, warm and steady.
