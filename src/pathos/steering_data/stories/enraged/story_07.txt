The brass trophy glinted under the ceremony lights, too heavy in the hands of the man who’d just been presented with the Lifetime Achievement Award in his field. His knuckles whitened on the cold metal, sweat stinging his eyes despite the chilled air. "This is the pinnacle," the presenter murmured smoothly, voice like polished stone, "a testament to your extraordinary contribution." The words landed like stones in the still pond of his mind, but his chest felt like it was being crushed
