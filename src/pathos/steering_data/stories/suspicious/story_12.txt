My fingers brushed the cover of the book I’d just bought at the thrift store, the cardboard brittle under my touch. Inside, pages crinkled like dry leaves. Then, the familiar, slightly frayed corner of a cheap paperback – *The Adventures of Tom Sawyer* – yielded the letter. My breath hitched, cold as the book’s spine. The ink was faded, the paper thin, smelling of damp earth and time. Who had written this? Why was it hidden here, deep in the book’s spine? My pulse hammered against my ribs, a frantic bird trapped in my chest. The word 'love' appeared in the faded script, but the name beneath it felt like a ghost. I turned the page slowly, the paper trembling in my hand
