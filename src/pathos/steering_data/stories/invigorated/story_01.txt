The coffee line stretched like a slow river under the cafe’s low ceiling. Maya watched the barista’s hands move with practiced ease, the steam from her own cold cup curling upward like a forgotten sigh. Then, the stranger ahead of her—someone she hadn’t noticed before—reached into their pocket with a swift, decisive motion. The crisp *click* of the coins landing in the counter felt like a jolt. Before she could even process the action, the stranger’s fingers closed on the cup, warm and heavy against her palm.
