The email blinked on the screen, the words *scholarship application denied* stark against the pale background. Maya’s fingers trembled as she scrolled back, the screen’s cold light reflecting in her suddenly wide eyes. Her breath hitched, a sharp intake followed by a slow, shallow exhalation that felt like smoke escaping her lungs. The room seemed to shrink, the unfamiliar clutter of her bedroom pressing in, the silence louder than the buzzing in her ears. She’d spent months crafting this, every late night, every revised essay, every call to the committee – now it felt like a stone in her stomach, heavy and unyielding.

She dropped the laptop, the soft thud echoing in the sudden quiet. Her thumb brushed the edge of the paper rejection notice, the crisp edge biting into her skin.
