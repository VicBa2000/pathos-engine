When I bent to look, my hands trembled so badly I almost dropped the drawing. The eyes were too big, too perfectly drawn, like the ones in the old photos of my childhood. My throat tightened, a tight band of heat rising in my chest. "Who’s that?" I whispered, my voice hoarse.
