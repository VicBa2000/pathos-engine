My fingers hovered over the crumpled drawing paper, the crayon smudges on Lily’s sketch of Mr. Whiskers – my childhood rabbit with perpetually startled eyes and a tail that seemed to flicker between solid and ghostly. The scent of lemon polish from Lily’s fingers mixed with the faint, dry smell of the paper. A warmth spread through my chest, light and slow, like sunlight finding a crack in the pavement. My shoulders relaxed, the tension in my neck
