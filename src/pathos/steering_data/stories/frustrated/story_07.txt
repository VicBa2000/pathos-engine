Third-person narration described the site: foundations swallowed by earth, skeletal remains of the porch leaning precariously. *This was the house*, he thought, the memory sharp and sudden, the one with the warm kitchen and the smell of baking bread. He dropped the keys, their metallic chime harsh against the silence. His jaw tightened, a familiar ache starting in his temples, as he remembered the last time he’d walked this path, the way the grass felt under his worn shoes.
