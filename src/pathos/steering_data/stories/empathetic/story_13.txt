The patient—small, with eyes the same soft brown as her late brother’s—lay still, breathing shallowly. Her own chest tightened, a familiar ache spreading from her ribs down her spine. She remembered the way her brother used to rest his head against her shoulder, the quiet rhythm of his breathing.
