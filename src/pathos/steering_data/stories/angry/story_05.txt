Now the chair was empty, the silence in the room thick and sudden, pressing against my eardrums. My chest tightened, a stone lodged deep, making it hard to draw air. The letter didn’t say *goodbye*; it simply stated the fact with finality, the kind of silence that feels like a door slammed shut in the middle of the night.
