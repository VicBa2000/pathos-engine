The announcement landed like a stone in his chest. "Your transfer to the Accounts department is effective next week," the manager said, her voice flat, avoiding his eyes. He hadn’t even finished the coffee he’d been holding, the warmth long gone. His hands, usually steady, shook as he pushed the paper aside, the crisp edge cutting into his palm. *This isn’t a promotion. This isn’t even a change.* The words echoed in the quiet office, louder than the hum of the fluorescent lights.
