My fingers were numb on the strings as the first chord echoed in the cavernous hall, the only sound the frantic hammering of my own heart against my ribs. The stage lights felt like cold coins against my skin, and the silence pressed down, thick and suffocating, pressing in from the empty space where the audience should have been. *Why did I believe this would be different?* The thought was a shard of glass cutting through my chest. I played on, the notes trembling with a desperation that had nothing to do with the music, the taste of metal sharp in my mouth.
