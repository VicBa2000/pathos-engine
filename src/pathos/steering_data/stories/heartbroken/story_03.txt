Outside, rain streaks the window, blurring the streetlights into streaks of gold. He stares at his reflection in the glass – eyes hollow, jaw clenched, a familiar tremor in his hands he can’t quite still. "Why?" he whispers to the empty space where the mirror should be, the question hanging heavier than the rain.
