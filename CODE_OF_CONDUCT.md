# Code of Conduct

## Pathos Engine Community Guidelines

### The Short Version

Be respectful. Be constructive. Focus on the work. We're building something interesting together.

### The Longer Version

This project exists to advance the understanding of functional emotional architectures for AI. Everyone who participates — through code, issues, discussions, or any other channel — is expected to:

**Do:**
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Show empathy toward other community members
- Credit others' work and ideas

**Don't:**
- Use sexualized language or imagery
- Engage in personal attacks, insults, or trolling
- Harass anyone publicly or privately
- Publish others' private information without permission
- Engage in any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channel associated with Pathos Engine.

### Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed on a best-effort basis.

Participants who violate this Code of Conduct may have their contributions removed and their access restricted at the maintainer's discretion.
